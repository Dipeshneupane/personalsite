Bank Loan Report(SQL + Tableau)

The Bank Loan Report project is designed to enhance the management and understanding of bank loan data through meticulous data validation using SQL and compelling data visualizations using Tableau. This project aims to ensure data accuracy and provide insightful visualizations to facilitate informed decision-making within the banking institution.

Key Objectives:

1. SQL(Data Validation):
  • Implement thorough data validation checks to ensure the accuracy and integrity of the bank loan data.
  • Verify data consistency, completeness, and adherence to predefined business rules.
  • Identify and rectify anomalies, outliers, and discrepancies in the loan dataset.
    • 2. Tableau(Data Visualization):
    • Utilize Tableau's powerful visualization tools to create interactive dashboards and reports.
    • Develop visualizations to represent loan approval rates, repayment trends, and risk assessments.
    • Design intuitive charts and graphs to showcase key performance indicators and insights derived from the loan data.
